Description

Submarine sandwhiches, or simply subs, are an easy to make, yet delicious and reliable meal. They typically consist of sliced deli meat, cheese, sauces, and vegetables in between a loaf of bread, along with with many choices for each of these categories.

Ingredients

  • Loaf of Bread
    • White Bread
    • Whole Wheat Bread
    • Five Grain Bread
  • Deli Meat
    • Turkey
    • Ham
    • Roast Beef
  • Cheese
    • Swiss
    • American
    • Provolone
    • Cheddar
    • Gouda
  • Sauce
    • Ranch Dressing
    • Chipotle Maynnoaise
    • Mustard
    • Maynnoaise
    • Barbecue Sauce
    • Ketchup (ew)
  • Vegetable Toppings
    • Lettuce
    • Pickles
    • Tomato Slices
    • Black Olives
    • Banana Peppers
    • Jalapeño Peppers
    • Onions
    • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Choose a type of bread and optionally use a base sauce (i.e. mayo).
  2. Choose a meat and cheese.
  3. (Optional) Toast the sandwich.
  4. Add vegetable toppings and additional sauces.
