Lasagna

Home

Description

Lasagna is a classic dish consisting of layers of pasta and filling made up of sauce and cheese. Diferent meats, such as ground beef or italian sausage, can also be included to add flavor and rich textures. This dish includes ragù and mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Sausage and ground beef
  • Crushed tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Cheese
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
    • Parmesan
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Cook the meat, make a sauce, and prepare cheesy fillings.
  3. Make the layers. Place sauce, noodles, then cheese.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 50 minutes (25 covered, 25 uncovered).
  5. Let the lasagna rest before slicing.
